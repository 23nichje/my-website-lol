What is a shiny?

A Shiny Pokeon is a pokemon that is a different color than normal, here is an example.

Galarian Zigzagoon Shiny Galarian Zigzagoon

So the normal chance of getting a shiny by just running into the pokemon is 1 in 4096

There is an item you can obtain from the doctor in the Chirchester hotel room called the Shiny Charm,the only way to obtain this item is to complete your pokedex, that means you have to catch every pokemon

The Masuda Method

The masuda method is a method where you take 2 pokemon, one from one language, and one from another, the only way for this to work is if that is the original language, you cannot have an english game and just change the keyboareds language

the rates for the Masuda Method are as follows, 1 in 682.7 and 1 in 512 with the Shiny Charm.
